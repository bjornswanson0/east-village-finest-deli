The Corner Store

A Neighborhood Fixture

Some places just hold a block together. East Village Finest Deli is one of them — the corner spot on Avenue B where the coffee is already brewing before sunrise and the grill is still going after most kitchens have closed.

It’s the full deal: a proper deli counter, a coffee bar, fresh juices, and shelves of groceries for everything you forgot. Sandwiches built to order, egg platters that fix a rough morning, and a menu deep enough that you could eat here every day for a month without repeating yourself. Plenty of regulars do.

No fuss, no lines out the door for the ’gram — just fast hands, friendly faces, and fair prices, seven days a week.
